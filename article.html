United Robots partner with Polish Academy of Sciences to produce an autonomous AI robot leading the fight against COVID-19

Sandeep Ram • Chief Commercial Officer

In accordance with our mission and core competence, we have been working with United Robots since September 2019. Our interest in this collaboration stemmed from their desire to use our expertise in the field of sensor integration, AI and autonomous robotics.

We are particularly proud to have been a key component in their latest product that is at the forefront of the fight against COVID-19 by harnessing the power of UV-C light to create a safer working environment” said Jacek Szklarski (PhD), Department of Intelligent Technologies IPPT PAN. “The alliance between our two organisations has produced a leading edge solution that will have a far reaching social and commercial impact” said Sandeep Ram, Chief Commercial Officer United Robots.

The Institute of Fundamental Technological Research is one of the biggest technical institutes of the Polish Academy of Sciences. They are a team of researchers and high-class specialists who want to add value to the knowledge of our society and sustainable development. By aiming at solid, recognizable scientific achievements, we are committed to initiate, develop and strengthen the cooperation between the most prominent representatives of various parts of the economy and outstanding scientists in the fields of technology.

About United Robots

United Robots develop autonomous intelligent robots designed to address the key problems facing service providers operating in vast areas such as supermarkets, warehouses, hospitals, care homes, office buildings and airports. Our robots have the ability to evaluate their surroundings and make informed decisions regarding navigation, mapping and task execution. We successfully apply expertise in mobile localisation, machine learning and computer vision to convert machines into intelligent AI devices capable of working without human supervision. Our solutions have been created by highly skilled and experienced R&D engineers and academics with years of experience in a field of robotics.
